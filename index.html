Happiest Birthday Madam Ji 🩷

You are one of the most real, kind, and extraordinary people I've ever had the luck of knowing. The pretty soul that entered into my life and made it memorable 💞 yes it's you, Madam Ji....

Khar sangaycha tar, tu khup special ahes maza life madhe, mhanje genuinely evdhi chaan cute best friend milali na mala, ki me kharach swatla lucky manto ki I have you...🙂

Kuthlihi vel asu de, kasahi mood asu de, tu always maza sathi astes kayam. Maza sobat kahi pn zhala tar pahila msg keva call tulach asto, ani tu pn kadhi "not interested", "nako na ata" mhanat nahis. He kharach khup matter karta maza sathi.

Tu khupach helpful ahes shembede 🤩 helpful mhanaya peksha mee tula lady luck 🤞 manel — yes, tu ahes maza sathi lady luck. Kevha, kuthe, kuthlihi ashirwad le ani kaam chalu ho jaayel...😁

Madam Ji, apan doghe sobat kiti masti keli ahe, kiti vedya goshti keli ahe 😅, kiti hasloy ki shevti pot dukhayla laagla mhanaycha vaa 🥹 he saglе moments mala kadhich visarayche nahit. Pudhcha vel maza mahit nahi kasa asel, kay asel, pn apan kayam sobat asu he mala mahit ahe... I am just one call away.

Tu sobat asles na, ki saglya vibes positive positive houn jatat 😁 ani special thank you tula, malaa ratri zopnyacha vel ase kiti pn kaam asel, kevha nustach apan jar 8 la bhetlo asel tarihi tu maza call 10 la nakki uchaltes... such a great thing 🤩

Ha, ani to photo me ka attach kela ahe tar — I love clicking pictures of you and storing them in my memories and mind 🤭 aaj paryant tujhe khup chaan chaan bhari bhari photos kadhle ahet, pn ha photo nehmi pahila ki maza face var ek smile yete, mast, I just love this one 💞

Tula khup tras deto na mee shembede 😁😁 pn kay karu, lagto thoda tras 😅. Just because, happy always — mala fakta tujha happy ani smile wala face baghaycha ahe 👀

Tasa tuza sathi bolayla khup kahi ahe, pn he WhatsApp wale na... yancha tar....

Bas evdhach sangaycha ahe ki tu best ahes, tu great ahes, ani I deserve all the good 😊. Me nehmi asel tuza sathi kayam... Thank you for everything Madam Ji 🩷, so lucky to have you in my life 😀

& very much proud of you, my decision maker 🤩

Love you & miss you a lot 🫶
